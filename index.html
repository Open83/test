I specialize in Payment Systems (Stripe, WorldPay) and Compliance Logic (HMRC Gift Aid, GDPR). I ensure that financial transactions and data handling are secure and bug-free.

Both. I use manual testing for complex business logic and exploratory scenarios, and I use automation (Postman, Selenium basics) for regression and API testing.

I use Jira for tracking. Every bug report includes Steps to Reproduce, Severity, Environment details, and Screenshots/Video evidence to ensure developers can fix it quickly.
